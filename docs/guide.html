Leader's Guide

How to read the dashboard, act on alerts, and keep the roster up to date.

Quick Reference
⚠️ Someone is on Kick Watch or Demotion Watch
The alert strip at the top of the HQ page lists who's at risk. Take action in-game, then message Boomer so the roster reflects the change.
📊 Stats need updating after an operation
Screenshot the Grand Total screen (all pages) and the My Task Force member list (all pages), then send them to Boomer on Discord.
👤 Someone joined, left, or changed rank
Message Boomer with the player's name and what happened. He'll update the roster, log it in Recent Changes, and move kicked members to the Graveyard.

Demotion Watch
An Officer whose participation has fallen below 80%. Give one warning. If it doesn't improve by the next stats refresh, demote them to Member in-game and notify Boomer.
⚠️
Kick Watch
A Member whose participation has fallen below 80%. Per squad rules, 3+ missed attacks in a single week is an automatic kick. Check their recent activity before acting.
Note: New recruits with zero recorded attacks are excluded from watch alerts — they don't have enough history for the stat to be meaningful yet.

Using the Roster

Intel, Attacks, and Participation are all-time Grand Totals — not this week's numbers. Use the sort buttons to surface the most useful view for your current task.

SortBest used when…
IntelDefault view. Shows overall long-term contribution ranked highest to lowest.
VPChecking who's climbing the Victory Points leaderboard.
Part.Quickly spotting who's been consistently active vs. coasting.
LevelChecking player levels against entry requirements.
⭐ PromoteSeeing who has earned an Officer promotion (500+ intel, 90%+ participation).
📈 Promo SoonIdentifying Members approaching the 500 intel promotion threshold.
⚠️ WatchReviewing everyone below 80% participation sorted to the top.
Tip: Combine the Role filter (Officers / Members) with the ⚠️ Watch sort to zero in on a specific rank without scrolling the full 44-person roster.
Submitting a Stats Update

Stats are refreshed manually after operations or when significant events happen. Here's how to kick one off:

1
Open Task Force Command → High Scores → Grand Total. Screenshot every page of the list (usually 4 screenshots).
2
Open Task Force Command → My Task Force. Screenshot every page of the member list — this captures VP, level, and role.
3
Send all screenshots to Boomer on Discord. He'll extract the numbers and push the update.
4
The site updates within minutes. Do a hard refresh (Ctrl + Shift + R on desktop, pull-to-refresh on mobile) if you don't see changes right away.
What gets updated: VP, level, intel, attacks, participation, missed attacks, member count, force points, and weekly intel. Role changes, new joins, and graveyard additions are logged separately.
Logging Roster Events
🆕
New member joined
Tell Boomer the player name. They'll be added to the roster with a New Recruit badge and logged in Recent Changes at the bottom of the page.
👋
Member left or was kicked
Tell Boomer the name and reason (left / kicked). Their final stats are preserved permanently in the Graveyard section and logged in Recent Changes.
⬆️
Promotion or demotion
Make the change in-game first, then tell Boomer the name and new role. A Promoted or Demoted badge will appear on their roster card and the event is logged.
🧟
Someone came back (Zombie)
Let Boomer know. They're re-added to the active roster with the Zombie badge to mark that they've returned from the dead.

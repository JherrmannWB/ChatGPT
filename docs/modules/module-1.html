AI Fundamentals

This module introduces artificial intelligence, what it is, what it is not, and how it applies to modern professional services.

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence refers to systems capable of perception, reasoning, decision-making, and learning from data.

What AI is NOT

  • Not sentient
  • Not magic
  • Not a replacement for human judgment

Core Capabilities

Natural Language Processing

Understanding and generating human language.

Pattern Recognition

Identifying trends and anomalies in data.

Machine Learning

Learning from historical data to make predictions.

Generative AI

Creating new content based on learned patterns.

Types of AI

Narrow AI

Task-specific systems (today’s reality).

General AI

Theoretical human-level intelligence.

Super AI

Hypothetical intelligence beyond humans.
